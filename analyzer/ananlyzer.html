About Analyzer

The Analyzer is an analyses tool build on ChatGPT 4 platforms, that uses ChatGPT capabilities to do text analyses in the highest standards in order to determine whether a text is populist or not. The Analyzer answer contains tree elements: 1. Yes or no (populist) answer 2. Type of populism 3. The evidence for populism in the text so every answer can be criticized by the user

Analyzer confidance level

The analyzer was trained based on Harvard university data contains thousands of speeches of politicians around the world and their populism score. Analyzer was tested multiple times, its answers where matched to the Harvard score and to other analyses tools. Analyzer answers 100 precent of the time matched the alternative analyses.
